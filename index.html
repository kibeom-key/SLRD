Privacy Policy

SlowReading (hereinafter "the app") values your privacy and complies with applicable laws.

Data Collection and Use

  1. No Personal Data Collection

    This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information (name, contact, location, device identifier, etc.).

  2. Logs and Analytics

    This app does not use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) and does not track your usage.

  3. Data Storage

    All data generated within the app (e.g., settings, records) is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to external servers. Data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Sharing

Since this app does not collect personal information, it does not share or outsource personal data to third parties under any circumstances.
